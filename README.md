# Task 2: Data Visualization and Storytelling

## Objective

To create visualizations that communicate meaningful business insights using the Superstore dataset.

## Tools Used

* Power BI
* Microsoft Excel

## Dataset

* Sample Superstore Dataset

## Dashboard Components

* Total Sales KPI
* Total Profit KPI
* Total Orders KPI
* Sales Trend Analysis
* Sales by Category
* Profit by Category
* Sales by Region
* Sales by Segment
* Top Products by Sales
* Sales by Sub-Category

## Key Insights

1. Total sales reached 2.30M.
2. Total profit reached 286.40K.
3. Technology generated the highest sales and profit.
4. Consumer customers contributed more than 50% of total sales.
5. Phones and Chairs were among the top-selling sub-categories.
6. The business processed approximately 5,009 orders.

## Outcome

Built an interactive Power BI dashboard and gained practical experience in data visualization, dashboard design, and business storytelling.
